03 : About me

UI/UX Designer | Backend Developer

The desire for UI / UX design came from a random thought. As a restaurant owner, a few years ago I started going around the kitchen preparing and assisting employees with food preparation. In addition to the orders, our visitors often had a problem with the prices they could not remember and the ingredients that were in each meal. So let's say I came up with the idea that maybe just as I was helping employees, it's time to help customers as well. After a long research I learned about UI / UX design and the whole process. I am totally in love! From thinking to research, to creating various paths and the goal of facilitating the user in the ordering / buying process, etc ..
All of this fits into my previous knowledge of programming and the combination of programming with research and design was phenomenal!
